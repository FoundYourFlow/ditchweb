FAQ

What can Ditch do?

Ditch handles reminders, check-ins, scheduling, web research, and analysis across images, PDFs, spreadsheets, presentations, and voice memos in one chat.

Are my messages private and secure?

Yes. Ditch is built with privacy-first practices, encrypted communication, and controlled data handling with user memory controls.

Are my payments secure?

Yes. Payments are processed securely through Stripe. Ditch does not directly store your full card details.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel anytime. Your plan remains active through the end of the current billing period, with no long-term lock-in.

How is my data privacy protected?

Ditch uses privacy-first data practices, stores only what is needed to deliver the service, and gives users control over memory and stored context.
